Assignment 2: Interactive Visualization on Top US Precipitation and Wind Speeds (2017)

This visualization shows the max precipitation and wind speed (WSF5) across the different states in the United States for 2017.

Key insight: Readers are able to extract tropical storms and hurricane instances through the map according to the level of precipitation. The data that is present is only for the months of January till Septermber of 2017.
